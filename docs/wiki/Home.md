# Welcome to the SHRC Member Highlights Wiki

This Wiki serves as the central documentation for the **Seoul Hanmaum Rotary Club (SHRC) Member Highlights System**. 

The system is designed to streamline how we collect, manage, and share the professional achievements of our members in our monthly newsletters.

## 📌 Navigation Guides

### 🙋 For Members
*   **[Member Guide](Member-Guide)**: Learn how to submit your highlights, upload photos, and update your information.

### 🛠️ For PR / Marketing Chairs
*   **[Admin Dashboard Guide](Admin-Guide)**: Instructions on how to review submissions and manage the highlights list.
*   **[ClubRunner Integration](ClubRunner-Integration)**: Step-by-step guide on exporting highlights and pasting them into the ClubRunner newsletter editor.

### 💻 For Tech Maintainers
*   **[Maintenance & Security](Maintenance-and-Security)**: Technical details on managing the Firebase project and authorized admin emails.
