# Admin Guide: Managing Highlights

The **PR and Marketing Chair** is responsible for curate the submitted highlights and preparing the monthly newsletter content.

## 1. Accessing the Dashboard
*   Log in with an authorized email address (Contact the Tech Committee if you need access).
*   Click on **"Admin Dashboard"** in the top navigation bar.
*   If you don't see this button, your email is not in the `ADMIN_EMAILS` list.

## 2. Dashboard Features

### The Submission List
All recent member submissions are displayed here. You can see:
*   The member's profile photo.
*   Their professional title and company.
*   The **Highlight Title** (new).
*   The **Highlight Body** with formatting (Bold/Links) preserved.
*   **Submission Date**: When the member entered their update.

## 3. Organizing Submissions
The dashboard includes advanced tools to help you plan your newsletter:

### Search & Filter
*   **Search Bar**: Type a member's name or club to instantly filter the list.
*   **Status Filter**: Toggle between **Pending** (new submissions) and **Archived** (past highlights you've already featured).

### Sorting
*   **Date Sort**: Arrange by "Newest First" to see the latest updates, or "Oldest First" to clear out older entries.
*   **Name Sort**: Alphabetize members for easier categorization.
*   **Asc/Desc Toggle**: Click the arrow icon to reverse the sorting direction.

### Archiving
Once a submission has been featured in a newsletter, click **"Archive Submission"** at the bottom of the card. 
*   This hides the entry from the "Pending" list.
*   Archived entries are preserved in the system and can be viewed or "Restored" at any time by switching the Status Filter.

## 4. Exporting to ClubRunner
*   Once you have selected the desired members and chosen the language, click **"Copy ClubRunner HTML"**.
*   A confirmation alert will appear: *"ClubRunner HTML copied to clipboard!"*
*   You are now ready to paste this into the ClubRunner email editor.
